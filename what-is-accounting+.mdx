Accounting+ is an accounting application used to record and manage various company financial transactions in a systematic and structured way. It provides features such as creating and managing master data (banks, taxes, currencies, bank accounts, vendors, customers, etc.), recording transactions like purchase invoices, journal entries, and generating financial reports such as income statements, balance sheets, trial balances, cash book reports, and more.

The application helps automate financial recording, reporting, and management, making it easier for businesses to monitor their financial condition and comply with accounting standards and audit requirements. It also includes search, filter, and AI assistance features to simplify usage and financial data analysis.

In short, Accounting+ is a digital and integrated accounting tool designed to efficiently and accurately handle business financial management, transaction recording, data management, and financial reporting.
